Meet The Drapery Lady

Christine St. Clair

Christine St. Clair is the daughter of an international businessman, who grew up in several different countries on three different continents of the world. It was commonplace for Christine to select fabrics from the local markets and take them to her seamstress to sew her clothing and home goods. In 1996, Christine was employed by one of San Jose's oldest drapery and shutter houses where she worked her way up to lead designer. It was here that she received the majority of her experience designing and selling all types of custom draperies, blinds, shades, and shutters for commercial establishments and some of Silicon Valley's finest homes. After eleven years, it was time that the student left the master and ventured out on her own into the next phase of her career. In June of 2009, Christine opened her own business as "The Drapery Lady."

Eventually, Christine returned to the United States. In 1985, while attending college, she sold ready-made draperies and blinds in a large department store to earn spending money. She enjoyed working with window coverings so much that after she completed her Bachelor of Arts, she moved into the department store's custom decorating department. The training Christine received at the department stores provided the basis of her window covering design experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Long Does The Consultation Take?

The average consultation lasts 2 hours. Larger homes usually require longer consultations.

Where Are Your Draperies Made?

Draperies, valances and Roman shades are fabricated in California.

Where Are Your Blinds, Shades and Shutters Made?

They are made in the U.S.A., Mexico and China.

I have my fabric. Will you work with my own fabric?

In most cases we can work with your own fabric.

How long does it take to have custom window treatments made?

The time that it takes to complete an order is dependent upon the time of year but for most of the year this is how long it takes for an order to be completed:

  • Custom draperies, valances, Roman shades, bedding and accessories take four to six weeks from the time of placing your order to the time of installation.
  • Custom plantation shutters take eight to ten weeks from the time of placing your order to the time of installation.
  • Custom blinds and shades take two to four weeks from the time of placing your order to the time of installation.

What is the difference between ready–made draperies and custom made draperies?

Ready-made draperies are mass produced in a factory and are available in a limited number of fabrics, colors, styles and sizes. Custom made draperies and window treatments are like getting a custom suit made specifically for your window. There are a large variety of fabrics, colors and styles that are available to select from and they are sized specifically for your windows. Please note that there are higher standards that are used in making custom draperies and window treatments as well.

Who does your installation?

The installation is done by professional installers who are licensed, bonded and insured.
